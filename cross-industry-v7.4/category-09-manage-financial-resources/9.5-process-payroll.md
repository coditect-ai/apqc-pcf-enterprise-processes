# 9.5 - Process payroll

> **PCF ID:** 10732 | **Elements:** 22 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 4

## Definition

Handling reporting time, managing pay, and processing taxes from salaries. Pay employees. Withhold taxes. Confirm the correct funds are paid to the correct government agency.

## Overview

This process group covers **process payroll** within the broader context of Manage Financial Resources.

### 9.5.2 - Manage pay

**PCF ID:** 10754

**Definition:** Managing the total payments made in employees payroll, including bonuses and compensation.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 9.5.2.1 | Enter employee time worked into payroll system | 10858 | 0 | N |

| 9.5.2.5 | Process and distribute payments | 10862 | 0 | N |
| 9.5.2.6 | Process and distribute manual checks | 10863 | 0 | N |
| 9.5.2.7 | Process period-end adjustments | 10864 | 0 | N |
| 9.5.2.8 | Respond to employee payroll inquiries | 10865 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.2.1 - Enter employee time worked into payroll system</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Tracking the number of hours worked for the payroll system. Register the number of hours worked by an employee into the payroll system for the purpose of calculating salaries or wages.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.2.2 - Maintain and administer employee earnings information</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Tracking and oversee salary breakups of employees. This process requires the organization to manage and update information pertaining to the structure of every employee's salary. This would involve the updating any changes to the salary structures of the employees, in a central repository which can be accessed by pertinent departments.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.2.3 - Maintain and administer applicable deductions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Processing salary deductions for tax purposes. Keep and manage the details of every employee's salary deductions based on their expenses and investments during the year.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.2.4 - Monitor changes in tax status of employees</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Tracking changes in the salary structure of employees for tax deductions. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.2.5 - Process and distribute payments</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Processing and distributing salaries to all employees. Execute the payroll management function through the dispensation of employee salaries. Leverage a centralized database of all payroll expenses.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.2.6 - Process and distribute manual checks</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Handling incorrect/omitted salary payments. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.2.7 - Process period-end adjustments</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Adjusting salary deductions for tax purposes at the end of the year. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.2.8 - Respond to employee payroll inquiries</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Addressing salary-related queries raised by employees. 


</details>


### 9.5.3 - Manage and process payroll taxes

**PCF ID:** 10755

**Definition:** Deducting and paying taxes from employees' salaries.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 9.5.3.1 | Develop tax plan | 14075 | 0 | N |
| 9.5.3.2 | Manage tax plan | 14076 | 0 | N |
| 9.5.3.3 | Calculate and pay applicable payroll taxes | 10866 | 0 | N |
| 9.5.3.4 | Produce and distribute employee annual tax statements | 10867 | 0 | N |
| 9.5.3.5 | File regulatory payroll tax forms | 10868 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.3.1 - Develop tax plan</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Devising a method to minimize payroll tax liability by means of allowances, deductions, exclusions or exemptions.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.3.2 - Manage tax plan</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Overseeing maintaining the reduction of payroll taxes [14075].


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.3.3 - Calculate and pay applicable payroll taxes</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Paying tax according to appropriate deductions made from salaries. Calculate and pay the tax liabilities according to the salaries and tax regulations of employees with the help of certified chartered accountants.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.3.4 - Produce and distribute employee annual tax statements</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Providing tax deductions statements created by certified chartered accountants to every employee for their reference or refunds.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.5.3.5 - File regulatory payroll tax forms</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Filling taxes, and highlighting different sources of income and expenditures made.


</details>
