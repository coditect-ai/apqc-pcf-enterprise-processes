# 9.4 - Manage fixed-asset project accounting

> **PCF ID:** 10731 | **Elements:** 12 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 3

## Definition

Managing accounts for large funds-invested projects. Manage and account for fixed assets projects (capital projects), which required significant capital investments over many years.

## Overview

This process group covers **manage fixed-asset project accounting** within the broader context of Manage Financial Resources.

### 9.4.1 - Perform capital planning and project approval

**PCF ID:** 10751

**Definition:** Preparing a project finance report to solicit approvals in capital projects. Prepare budgets for projects that require heavy investments. Report on project finances to solicit approvals from management.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 9.4.1.1 | Develop capital investment policies and procedures | 10844 | 0 | N |
| 9.4.1.2 | Develop and approve capital expenditure plans and budgets | 10845 | 0 | N |
| 9.4.1.3 | Review and approve capital projects and fixed-asset acquisitions | 10846 | 0 | N |
| 9.4.1.4 | Conduct financial justification for project approval | 10847 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>9.4.1.1 - Develop capital investment policies and procedures</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating procedures and policies to follow for investing in capital projects. Create rules and regulations regarding large investment plans, which require in-depth forecasting for expenditure and revenue.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.4.1.2 - Develop and approve capital expenditure plans and budgets</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating budgets, and soliciting approvals for capital projects. Prepare budgets for projects that require heavy investments. Secure approvals from management.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.4.1.3 - Review and approve capital projects and fixed-asset acquisitions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Evaluating and supporting capital investments in projects and fixed assets. Confirm details of capital projects. Secure approvals from managements for large investments.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.4.1.4 - Conduct financial justification for project approval</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Reviewing all project business cases in order to substantiate projected financial gains. Validate any project's business case. Juxtapose the benefits derived from moving a project forward against the associated costs. 


</details>


### 9.4.2 - Perform capital project accounting

**PCF ID:** 10752

**Definition:** Accounting for large-scale and large-cost investments. Manage and account for ongoing activities related to capital projects, including setting up new projects, recording project transactions, monitoring and tracking spending, closing and capitalizing projects, and measuring the financial returns on completed projects.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 9.4.2.1 | Create project account codes | 10848 | 0 | N |
| 9.4.2.2 | Record project-related transactions | 10849 | 0 | N |
| 9.4.2.3 | Monitor and track capital projects and budget spending | 10850 | 0 | N |
| 9.4.2.4 | Close/capitalize projects | 10851 | 0 | N |
| 9.4.2.5 | Measure financial returns on completed capital projects | 10852 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>9.4.2.1 - Create project account codes</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Giving reference codes for every project. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.4.2.2 - Record project-related transactions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Noting every transaction during a project in a common financial database. Document all transactions associated with any project. Maintain a centralized repository of all such financial data.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.4.2.3 - Monitor and track capital projects and budget spending</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Evaluating project progress and funds invested. Observe and track significant funds invested on any long-term project. Compare to budget.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.4.2.4 - Close/capitalize projects</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Checking for returns generated from projects for decision making. Evaluate capital projects that require heavy investments. Decide whether to proceed based on the revenues generated. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.4.2.5 - Measure financial returns on completed capital projects</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Comparing a finished project's profitability with forecasted returns. Scrutinize revenues generated by completed projects that required heavy investments. Determine profitability.


</details>
