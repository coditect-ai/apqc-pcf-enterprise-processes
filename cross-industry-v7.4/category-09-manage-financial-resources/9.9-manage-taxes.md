# 9.9 - Manage taxes

> **PCF ID:** 10736 | **Elements:** 13 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 2

## Definition

Estimating the organization's periodic tax liabilities. Ensure that appropriate taxing authorities receive tax return filings and payments when due.

## Overview

This process group covers **manage taxes** within the broader context of Manage Financial Resources.

### 9.9.1 - Develop tax strategy and plan

**PCF ID:** 10765

**Definition:** Setting targets for periodic tax liabilities. Assess the tax impact of various activities such as the acquisition or disposal of fixed assets or a deliberate change in number of employee.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |

<details>
<summary><strong>9.9.1.1 - Develop foreign, national, state, and local tax strategy</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Developing a tax strategy for foreign, national, state, local administration. Set up tax strategies for foreign trade in imports and exports and at national, state, and local level.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.9.1.2 - Consolidate and optimize total tax plan</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Combining and enhancing a rational analysis of a financial condition or plan from a tax perspective in order to align financial goals through efficient tax planning. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.9.1.3 - Maintain tax master data</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Maintaining a master file about the rational analysis of a financial condition or plan from a tax perspective in order to align financial goals through efficient tax planning. 


</details>


### 9.9.2 - Process taxes

**PCF ID:** 10766

**Definition:** Processing the taxes of the organization in line with the regional taxation structure, including corporate, property, excise, and service taxes.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 9.9.2.1 | Perform tax planning/strategy | 10930 | 0 | N |
| 9.9.2.2 | Prepare tax returns | 10931 | 0 | N |
| 9.9.2.3 | Prepare foreign taxes | 10932 | 0 | N |
| 9.9.2.4 | Calculate deferred taxes | 10933 | 0 | N |
| 9.9.2.5 | Account for taxes | 10934 | 0 | N |
| 9.9.2.6 | Monitor tax compliance | 10935 | 0 | N |
| 9.9.2.7 | Address tax inquiries | 10936 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>9.9.2.1 - Perform tax planning/strategy</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating and implementing strategies for taxes to be paid or collected by the business. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.9.2.2 - Prepare tax returns</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Preparing and submitting tax reports for every employee to the tax department in order to show the tax paid and deducted from their salaries in the year.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.9.2.3 - Prepare foreign taxes</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Preparing reports about paid or accrued foreign taxes to an overseas country.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.9.2.4 - Calculate deferred taxes</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Calculating the income that has been realized when the tax on that income has not.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.9.2.5 - Account for taxes</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Managing the organization's financial accounts for the purpose of taxation. Prepare and maintain the tax paid by the organization to the country they have business in.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.9.2.6 - Monitor tax compliance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Checking and correcting the tax policies according to the rules and regulations set by the organization.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.9.2.7 - Address tax inquiries</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Addressing any tax queries by any regulatory or government authorities. Review historical records related to taxation within the organization in order to respond to queries. 


</details>
