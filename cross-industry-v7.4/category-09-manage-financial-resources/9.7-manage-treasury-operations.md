# 9.7 - Manage treasury operations

> **PCF ID:** 10734 | **Elements:** 62 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 4

## Definition

Managing business's investments in trading in bonds, currencies, financial derivatives, etc. Manage the financial assets and holdings of the organization. Optimize the organization's liquidity. Invest excess cash. Reduce financial risks.

## Overview

This process group covers **manage treasury operations** within the broader context of Manage Financial Resources.

### 9.7.1 - Manage treasury policies and procedures

**PCF ID:** 10758

**Definition:** Managing rules and regulations for investments in trading in bonds, currencies, financial derivatives, etc. Establish policies and procedures for investments made. Optimize liquidity in treasury operations.

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.1.1 - Establish scope and governance of treasury operations</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Selecting opportunities and the authoritative body for investments in trading in bonds, currencies, financial derivatives, etc.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.1.2 - Establish and publish treasury policies</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating and providing investment regulations for the organization. Establish policies and procedures for investments to optimize liquidity in treasury operations. Create a written copy of it. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.1.3 - Develop treasury procedures</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Making processes for investing. Create steps for investments in bonds, currencies, and financial derivatives in order to optimize company's liquidity, invest excess cash, and reduce its financial risks.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.1.4 - Monitor treasury procedures</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Checking treasury processes in order to optimize company's liquidity, invest excess cash, and reduce its financial risks.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.1.5 - Audit treasury procedures</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Auditing the treasury function. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.1.6 - Revise treasury procedures</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Reassessing all treasury procedures based on audit findings. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.1.7 - Develop and confirm internal controls for treasury</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating and managing the internal control systems for investments in bonds, currencies, and financial derivatives to verify procedures.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.1.8 - Define system security requirements</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Describing the need of system security requirements for controlling access, reliability of information, accountability, and availability of information in the organization.


</details>

Develop cash flow forecasts. Manage cash flows. Produce cash management accounting transactions and reports. Manage and oversee banking relationships. <details>
<summary><strong>9.7.2.1 - Manage and reconcile cash positions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Correcting cash differences in the books of accounts. Make optimum utilization of funds available in the business. Check for differences to rectify. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.2.2 - Manage cash equivalents</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Taking care of all cash-related activities in the business. Utilize short-term assets that can be easily convertible into cash, such as marketable securities, commercial paper and short-term government bonds, and treasury bills.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.2.3 - Process and oversee electronic fund transfers (EFTs)</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Supervising all online transactions.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.2.4 - Develop cash flow forecasts</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Preparing forecasts for the cash generated or used by the organization. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.2.5 - Manage cash flows</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Delaying the outflow of funds as long as possible, but encourage the inflow of as fast as possible.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.2.6 - Produce cash management accounting transactions and reports</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Presenting reports on all cash-related activities. Collect and manage short-term investing activities. Prepare reports of all transactions done. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.2.7 - Manage and oversee banking relationships</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Maintaining and directing the course of relationships with banking partners.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.2.8 - Analyze, negotiate, resolve, and confirm bank fees</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Studying and finalizing bank fees for services provided by banks. Negotiate and finalize nominal fees that bank charges for various services, such as requesting a deposit slip or counter check or certifying papers.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.3.1 - Manage in-house bank accounts for subsidiaries</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Maintaining subsidiaries' company accounts opened with bank inside the corporation. Manage different financial services provided by in-house bank structure for parent companies' subsidiaries or branches. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.3.2 - Manage and facilitate inter-company borrowing transactions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Arranging loans for subsidiaries from in-house banks. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.3.3 - Manage centralized outgoing payments on behalf of subsidiaries</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Handling payments made for subsidiaries by parent company. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.3.4 - Manage central incoming payments on behalf of subsidiaries</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Handling payments received by parent company for subsidiaries.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.3.5 - Manage internal payments and netting transactions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Taking care of all business outflows and recording as whole. Manage making all payments for the organization and its units or subsidiaries. Track in books of accounts of parent company.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.3.6 - Calculate interest and fees for in-house bank accounts</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Computing all expenses paid to and receivables collected over the organization's banking activity. Calculate all charges and receivables, towards interest, fees, and any other payments over its own bank accounts. Record transactions in the books of accounts.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.3.7 - Provide account statements for in-house bank accounts</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Facilitating account statements for all in-house banking activity.


</details>

</details>

</details>

<summary><strong>9.7.4.3 - Manage liquidity</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Managing and maintaining enough liquidity in form of cash and cash equivalents in the business to meet urgent and timely requirements 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.4.4 - Manage issuer exposure</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Managing the exposure incurred by the issuer for providing credit to the borrower.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.4.5 - Process and oversee debt and investment transactions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Tracking loans taken and money invested in different options. Arrange and supervise loans from banks and individuals and investments in different available and profitable options.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.4.6 - Process and oversee foreign currency transactions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Arranging and supervising foreign exchange rate changes to avoid loss on foreign-currency transactions. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.4.7 - Produce debt and investment accounting transaction reports</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating transactions report of loans and investments. Prepare and maintain records of loans and investment transactions.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>9.7.4.8 - Process and oversee interest rate transactions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Supervising the interest paid or received by the organization. Arrange and supervise interest rate swap transactions to manage exposure to fluctuations in interest rates. Or attain a marginally lower rate of interest than could be gained through a swap. 


</details>
