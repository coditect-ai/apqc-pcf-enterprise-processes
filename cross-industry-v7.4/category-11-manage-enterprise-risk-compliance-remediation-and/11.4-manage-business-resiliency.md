# 11.4 - Manage business resiliency

> **PCF ID:** 11216 | **Elements:** 6 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 1

## Definition

Including the processes required to rapidly adapt and respond to any internal or external opportunity, demand, disruption, or threat. Develop a more dynamic, strategic, and integrated approach to managing compliance obligations.

## Overview

This process group covers **manage business resiliency** within the broader context of Manage Enterprise Risk, Compliance, Remediation, and Resiliency.

### 11.4.1 - Develop the business resilience strategy

**PCF ID:** 11221

**Definition:** Creating a strategy for rapidly adapting to disturbances. Maintain continuous business processes and protecting employees, assets, and overall brand equity.


### 11.4.2 - Perform continuous business operations planning

**PCF ID:** 11222

**Definition:** Developing plans to ensure continuous business operations. 


### 11.4.3 - Test continuous business operations

**PCF ID:** 11223

**Definition:** Assessing ongoing activities within the organization that are not intended to stop except for in an emergency. 


### 11.4.4 - Maintain continuous business operations

**PCF ID:** 11224

**Definition:** Evaluating business operations. Determine which activities generate revenues, perform best, and provide good returns.


### 11.4.5 - Share knowledge of specific risks across other parts of the organization

**PCF ID:** 16471

**Definition:** Sharing information about risks and resilience strategies of business operations across the organization so that prospective risks can be avoided.
