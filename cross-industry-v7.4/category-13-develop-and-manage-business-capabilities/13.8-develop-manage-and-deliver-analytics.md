# 13.8 - Develop, Manage, and Deliver Analytics

> **PCF ID:** 20959 | **Elements:** 6 | **Metrics Available:** N | **Benchmarkable:** 0

## Definition

Creating new and improving existing analytical capabilities to enhance data management pipeline. 

## Overview

This process group covers **develop, manage, and deliver analytics** within the broader context of Develop and Manage Business Capabilities.

### 13.8.1 - Develop and manage hypotheses

**PCF ID:** 20960

**Definition:** Creating theories that explain empirical data. Use the hypotheses to guide feature selection in the process of data collection.


### 13.8.2 - Collect data

**PCF ID:** 20961

**Definition:** Gathering and harvesting structured and unstructured data from disparate sources. Clean and pre-process data. Remove duplicates. Convert to a uniform format to make records comparable.


### 13.8.3 - Analyze data

**PCF ID:** 20962

**Definition:** Conducting data analysis. Choose statistical algorithms that best reveal patterns and trends in the data. Compare with hypotheses and time series forecasts. Determine error rates and significant outliers. 


### 13.8.4 - Report on data

**PCF ID:** 20963

**Definition:** Summarizing and documenting the results of data analysis. Create graphs and visualizations to illustrate numerical findings to make them more accessible to readers.


### 13.8.5 - Identify remedial actions

**PCF ID:** 20964

**Definition:** Determining the steps that need to be taken to correct the shortcomings.
