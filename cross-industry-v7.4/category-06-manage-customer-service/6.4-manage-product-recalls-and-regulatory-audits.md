# 6.4 - Manage product recalls and regulatory audits

> **PCF ID:** 20110 | **Elements:** 7 | **Metrics Available:** N | **Benchmarkable:** 0

## Definition

Removing defective products from the distribution chain. Participate in audits from watchdog agencies.

## Overview

This process group covers **manage product recalls and regulatory audits** within the broader context of Manage Customer Service.

### 6.4.1 - Initiate recall

**PCF ID:** 20111

**Definition:** Commencing the removal process of defective products.


### 6.4.2 - Assess the likelihood and consequences of occurrence of any hazards

**PCF ID:** 20112

**Definition:** Performing risk analysis. Identify all dangers, evaluate how probable they are, and what kinds of negative results or or adverse side effect they carry.


### 6.4.3 - Manage recall related communications

**PCF ID:** 20113

**Definition:** Handling communications that are related to product recalls.


### 6.4.4 - Submit regulatory reports

**PCF ID:** 20114

**Definition:** Creating and delivering reports to regulatory agencies to provide details about handling product recalls. 


### 6.4.5 - Monitor and audit recall effectiveness

**PCF ID:** 20115

**Definition:** Analyzing the effectiveness of product recalls.


### 6.4.6 - Manage recall termination

**PCF ID:** 20116

**Definition:** Ending product recalls, communicating to the public and filing reports.
