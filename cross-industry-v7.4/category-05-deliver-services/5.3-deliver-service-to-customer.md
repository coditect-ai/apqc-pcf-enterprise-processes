# 5.3 - Deliver service to customer

> **PCF ID:** 20058 | **Elements:** 27 | **Metrics Available:** N | **Benchmarkable:** 3

## Definition

Rendering service to the customer by initiating, executing, and completing tasks associated with service delivery.

## Overview

This process group covers **deliver service to customer** within the broader context of Deliver Services.

### 5.3.1 - Initiate service delivery

**PCF ID:** 20059

**Definition:** Collaborating with the customer to understand service needs. Review, understand, and modify the delivery scope with the organization needs of the customer in mind. Confirm readiness and identify, select, and assign resources. Plan for service delivery.

**Definition:** Meeting with the customer, partner, and/or supplier to review the terms of the solutions contract and agree on the terms set forth.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.1.2 - Understand customer requirements and define refine approach</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Taking the customer requirements for a solution and applying those requirements to a refined approach for service. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.1.3 - Modify/revise and approve project plan</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Updating the project plan to align with the new solution approach agreed upon with the customer.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.1.4 - Review customer business objectives</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Aligning the customer business objectives with the agreed service delivery solution.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.1.5 - Confirm environmental readiness</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Confirming that the organization has the recourses necessary to meet the expectations for the solution for service delivery.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.1.6 - Identify, select, and assign resources</strong> (2 tasks)</summary>

</details>

**Definition:** Establishing a plan of action to successfully render a solution for service delivery.


</details>


### 5.3.2 - Execute service delivery

**PCF ID:** 20069

**Definition:** Carrying out service delivery to the customer by creating and deploying the necessary solution. Analyze need and create a solution. Validate the solution and make changes if needed. Obtain approval to build/buy solution and then deploy solution to customer.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 5.3.2.1 | Analyze environment and customer needs | 20070 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.2.2 | Define solution | 20071 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.2.3 | Validate solution | 20072 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.2.4 | Identify changes | 20073 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.2.5 | Obtain approval to proceed | 20074 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.2.6 | Make build/buy solution | 20075 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.2.7 | Deploy solution | 20076 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.2.1 - Analyze environment and customer needs</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Understanding the needs of the customer and providing the necessary resources to meet those requirements within the scope of the organization. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.2.2 - Define solution</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating a plan of action to provide service delivery to the customer through a possible solution. This solution should be in response to a collaborative effort made by the organization and the customer to meet service delivery needs.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.2.3 - Validate solution</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Validating that the proposed solution is feasible and provides the needed services for the customer.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.2.4 - Identify changes</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Realizing issues within the original drafted solution and providing changes to correct those issues.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.2.5 - Obtain approval to proceed</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Gaining approval from all avenues to proceed with providing solutions for service delivery.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.2.6 - Make build/buy solution</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Constructing or purchasing solutions necessary to provide service delivery.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.2.7 - Deploy solution</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Providing the customer with promised services and solutions. 


</details>


### 5.3.3 - Complete service delivery

**PCF ID:** 20077

**Definition:** Implementing final steps to complete service delivery to the customer. Evaluate success through project review, complete finance activities, and confirm delivery. Release resources and manage completion by harvesting knowledge and systems by archiving records.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 5.3.3.1 | Conduct service delivery/project review and evaluate success | 20078 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.3.2 | Complete/finalize financial management activities | 20079 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.3.3 | Confirm delivery according to contract terms | 20080 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.3.4 | Release resources | 20081 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.3.5 | Manage service delivery completion | 20082 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.3.6 | Harvest knowledge | 20083 | 0 | N |
| 5.3.3.7 | Archive records and update systems | 20084 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.3.1 - Conduct service delivery/project review and evaluate success</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Reviewing the entire service delivery process to evaluate the success of the project from beginning to end.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.3.2 - Complete/finalize financial management activities</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Insuring all payments are received and all activates therein are completed. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.3.3 - Confirm delivery according to contract terms</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Confirming that the organization has satisfied all terms of the delivery contract set forth in collaboration between the organization and customer.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.3.4 - Release resources</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Discharging leveraged resources from service delivery commitments upon completion. Returning resources to the resource pool.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.3.5 - Manage service delivery completion</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Ensuring that all aspects of the service delivery process are completed both internally and externally.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.3.6 - Harvest knowledge</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Garnering feedback from all avenues to collect a knowledge base concerning services rendered.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>5.3.3.7 - Archive records and update systems</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Completing and archiving all records associated with requested services. Update all necessary systems to reflect those changes.


</details>
