# 8.2 - Develop and manage IT business strategy

> **PCF ID:** 20652 | **Elements:** 51 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 1

## Definition

Handling the business of IT. Create a organization-wide strategy for the IT function. Define the organization's IT architecture. Manage the IT portfolio. Research and innovate in the field of IT. Assess and convey the performance and the value of the IT function.

## Overview

This process group covers **develop and manage it business strategy** within the broader context of Manage Information Technology (IT).

### 8.2.1 - Define business technology and governance strategy

**PCF ID:** 20653

**Definition:** Defining the need of technology in business and systematic implementation of IT investments. It comprises of assessing competitive technology components to ensure structural analysis, development, usage and security of technology for efficient business operations.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 8.2.1.1 | Build and maintain IT strategic intelligence | 20654 | 0 | N |
| 8.2.1.2 | Monitor and map current and emerging technologies | 20655 | 0 | N |
| 8.2.1.3 | Define and communicate digital transformation strategy | 20656 | 0 | N |
| 8.2.1.4 | Develop IT strategic alignment | 20657 | 0 | N |
| 8.2.1.5 | Articulate IT alignment principles | 20658 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.1.1 - Build and maintain IT strategic intelligence</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Building and maintaining intelligence towards changing organizational goals, supporting management, and operational or functional levels of the business. It is the ability to understand business trends that present threats or opportunities for IT in an organization.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.1.2 - Monitor and map current and emerging technologies</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Monitoring and evaluating existing and forthcoming technologies to meet the current and future growth plans for business operations.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.1.3 - Define and communicate digital transformation strategy</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Defining the integration of digital technology into business operations and service delivery, and convey the strategy to different segments of business. It is always backed by continuous improvement followed by periodic review and change per requirement of the business.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.1.4 - Develop IT strategic alignment</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Developing the process of aligning the organization's business divisions and staff members with the organization's planned objectives for IT.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.1.5 - Articulate IT alignment principles</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Systematic approach to clearly communicate and operate the usage of information technology as it relates to business objectives.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.1.6 - Maintain IT strategic alignment</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Maintaining alignment of the organization's business divisions and staff members with the organization's planned objectives for IT.


</details>

**Definition:** Strategy for systematic management of IT investments, projects, and activities. Analyze and examine the value of the IT portfolio and allocate resources based on business objectives.

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.2.1 - Establish and validate IT value criteria</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Create and certify the standards to determine the value of the investments, projects, and activities of IT function for the overall business objectives.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.2.2 - Determine IT portfolio investment balance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Determining the uninvested amount out of the total approved amount for overall IT management, IT investments, projects, and activities.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.2.3 - Evaluate  proposed IT investment projects</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Evaluating IT investment projects to achieve overall business objectives in regard to implementation, efficiency, and profitability.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.2.4 - Prioritize IT projects</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Listing the IT projects in the order of most important to the least. Determining which of many IT projects are most important or critical to business operations.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.2.5 - Align IT resources to strategic priorities</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Aligning physical IT resources like software, IT infrastructure, networks, and non-physical resources like technology expertise, to strategic objectives ranked by their importance in achieving the strategic goals.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.2.6 - Align IT portfolio to business objectives</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Aligning IT investments, projects, and activities to achieve overall business objectives.


</details>


### 8.2.3 - Define and maintain enterprise architecture

**PCF ID:** 20668

**Definition:** Outlining and maintaining the organization's IT architecture. Establish the IT architecture definition and framework. Ensure the relevance of IT. Create and confirm the approach for IT maintenance. Create rules and regulations to guide IT architecture. Authenticate and finalize all IT related research and innovation that takes place within the organization.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 8.2.3.1 | Create and publish enterprise architecture principles | 20670 | 0 | N |
| 8.2.3.2 | Establish and operate enterprise architecture governance | 20671 | 0 | N |
| 8.2.3.3 | Research technologies to innovate IT services and solutions | 20672 | 0 | N |
| 8.2.3.4 | Provide input to definition and prioritization of IT projects | 20673 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.3.1 - Create and publish enterprise architecture principles</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating and publishing high level statements of the fundamental values (principles) based on the organization's objectives that guide Information Technology decision-making and activities, and are the foundation for enterprise architecture.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.3.2 - Establish and operate enterprise architecture governance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Establishing and operating a structure by which an enterprise defines appropriate strategies and ensures development alignment with those strategies. Create and establish the rules, regulations, policies, and standards that will govern the individual components of the IT architecture, as well as the architecture in its entirety.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.3.3 - Research technologies to innovate IT services and solutions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Systematically investigating and studying materials and sources relevant to the IT function. Reach meaningful insights and conclusions in the form of new ideas and innovation for delivering IT services and solutions.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.3.4 - Provide input to definition and prioritization of IT projects</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Analyze the value driven through IT projects and redefine and/or reprioritize. Evaluate planning, organizing, and implementation of IT projects based on research outcomes and business objectives.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.4.1 - Establish  IT service management strategy and goals</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Implementing strategy for designing, delivering, managing, and improving the way information technology be used in the organization. The goal of IT Service Management is to ensure that the right processes, people, and technology are in place to meet business goals.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.4.2 - Identify  IT service operating and process requirements</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Identifying operating and process requirement for designing, delivering, managing, and improving the way information technology be used in the organization.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.4.3 - Define IT service catalog</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Create and design an organized and curated collection of all IT-related services that can be performed by, for, or within the organization. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.4.4 - Establish IT service management framework</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Create a layered structure for IT service management framework ensuring right processes, people, and technology are in place to meet business goals.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.4.5 - Define and implement IT service management</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Defining and implementing activities involved in designing, creating, delivering, supporting, and managing the lifecycle of information technology services within an organization.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.4.6 - Define and deploy support service management process tools and methods</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Establishing services for providing support to users of IT services and solutions. Define the plethora of services along with tools and methods by which the organization assists users of computers, software products, or other electronic/mechanical products.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.4.7 - Monitor and report IT performance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Supervising, analyzing, and reporting performance of information technology to ensure they are on-course and on-schedule in meeting the organizational objectives and performance targets.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.5.1 - Determine IT performance measures</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Determining measures for evaluating the performance of IT services in the organization. Establish key performance indicators, including the IT services performance index.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.5.2 - Define IT control points and assurance procedures governance model</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Establishing a governance model with its own structure and functions from where full or partial control can be exercised over the entire IT management system. Specify evaluation and quality control procedures in the structure.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.5.3 - Monitor and analyze overall IT performance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Monitoring and analyzing information technology performance measures to ensure timely completion, and that quality assurance is met at all steps of IT services.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.5.4 - Monitor and analyze IT financial performance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Checking and analyzing predetermined financial targets and timelines of IT management system. Monitoring their profitability, feasibility, and consistency. Study the revenues generated.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.5.5 - Monitor and analyze IT value and benefits</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Examining and analyzing the value and benefits of IT service management to ensure benefits outweigh incurred costs.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.5.6 - Optimize IT resource allocation</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Create process to assign and manage IT assets that support organization's strategic goals.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.5.7 - Manage IT projects and services interdependencies</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Manage capabilities required for the successful delivery of information technology projects, which, by extension, affect the success of the overall IT services.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.5.8 - Report  IT service and project performance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Process of collecting, analyzing, and reporting information regarding the performance of IT services and projects. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.2.5.9 - Select, deploy, and operate IT performance analytics tools</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Select, establish, and operate analytics tool to analyze data and extract actionable and commercially relevant information on IT performance to evaluate or increase performance.


</details>
