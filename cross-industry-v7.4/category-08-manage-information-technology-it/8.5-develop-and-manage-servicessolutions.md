# 8.5 - Develop and manage services/solutions

> **PCF ID:** 20784 | **Elements:** 40 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 1

## Definition

Designing and maintaining the IT services/solutions catalogue. Evaluate the performance of IT services/solutions. Communicate the results to the management.

## Overview

This process group covers **develop and manage services/solutions** within the broader context of Manage Information Technology (IT).

<details>

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.1.3 - Identify, deploy, and support development methodologies and tools</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Identifying and implementing techniques and tools for development based on overall value addition to the IT environment.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.1.4 - Establish service component criteria</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Establishing standards for selection of IT service components.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.1.5 - Understand and select reusable service components</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Understanding and selecting reusable service components so that they can be cost-effective and efficient.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.1.6 - Maintain service component portfolio</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating and establishing service component portfolio by defining investments, and activities. Analyze and examine the value of the service component portfolio, and allocate resources towards it.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.1.7 - Establish development standards exception governance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating standards and procedures for developing IT services/solutions outside of defined business parameters.


</details>


### 8.5.2 - Manage service/solution lifecycle planning

**PCF ID:** 20793

**Definition:** Executing life-cycle planning for IT services and solutions. Develop new requirements and feature-function enhancements. Create and design a life cycle plan that addresses the current and future state of IT services and solutions.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 8.5.2.1 | Monitor and track emerging technology capabilities | 20794 | 0 | N |
| 8.5.2.2 | Identify IT services/solutions | 20795 | 0 | N |
| 8.5.2.3 | Determine IT service/solution approach | 20796 | 0 | N |
| 8.5.2.4 | Define IT solution lifecycle | 20797 | 0 | N |
| 8.5.2.5 | Develop IT service/solution "sunset" plans | 20798 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.2.1 - Monitor and track emerging technology capabilities</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Perform a systematic investigation to new and future technology capabilities for future upgrades. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.2.2 - Identify IT services/solutions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Identifying processes and supporting procedures that are performed by an organization to design, plan, deliver, operate, and control information technology services/solutions offered to customers.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.2.3 - Determine IT service/solution approach</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Determining an approach to create a base for delivering IT service/solution aligned with overall business needs while maintaining a tight control on delivery and costs.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.2.4 - Define IT solution lifecycle</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Defining solutions to satisfy business needs. IT solution lifecycle provides a means to address the full life cycle of an information technology solution and addresses the current and future state of IT services and solutions.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.2.5 - Develop IT service/solution "sunset" plans</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Developing plans to retire IT service/solution resources when the service/solution is no longer feasible.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.3.2 - Assess business constraints on IT service/solution</strong></summary>

<summary><strong>8.5.3.3 - Determine IT component integration requirements</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Determining the requirements to integrate IT components such as hardware, software, database, telecommunication, and network.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.3.4 - Identify opportunities for IT component reuse</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Identification of opportunities for reusing IT components so that they can be cost-effective and efficient.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.3.5 - Promote adoption of existing service/solution architecture</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Encouraging acceptance of existing IT service/solution architecture in the organization.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.3.6 - Develop and maintain service/solution architectures</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating and maintaining a services and solutions architecture over a network that can be revised as needed or even eliminated in case of inefficiencies.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.3.7 - Assess IT service/solution architecture conformance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Assessing functional compliance of the IT service/solution architecture. Safeguard compliance with guidelines for the architecture.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.5.3.8 - Manage architectural exceptions</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Identifying and resolving any architectural exceptions. Address the internal inquiries related to architecture that cannot be addressed immediately. Research inquiries that require the need of exceptional methods.


</details>
