# 8.6 - Deploy services/solutions

> **PCF ID:** 20824 | **Elements:** 42 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 1

## Definition

Executing IT services/solutions by creating a strategy for deployment. Plan and execute the changes. Plan and administer the release of its IT services and solutions.

## Overview

This process group covers **deploy services/solutions** within the broader context of Manage Information Technology (IT).

### 8.6.1 - Develop and manage service/solution deployment strategy

**PCF ID:** 20825

**Definition:** Creating and implementing a strategy for the deployment of IT service/solution. Define all of the activities that make the IT function available for use. Establish the change policies for IT services and solutions. Define the deployment process, procedures, and tools. Select the most feasible and practical methodologies for the deployment process.

|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|

| 8.6.1.3 | Define and create deployment procedure workflow | 20828 | 0 | N |
| 8.6.1.4 | Define IT change/release standards | 20829 | 0 | N |
| 8.6.1.5 | Assign deployment approval responsibilities | 20830 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.1.1 - Assess IT deployment business impact</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Evaluate the impact of IT deployment (products/services) on the business. Compare pre and post development performance, behavior of resources, and cost to assess organizational benefit.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.1.2 - Establish IT deployment policies</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Defining deployment policies regarding IT services and solutions to allow employees to plan accordingly. Reduce the negative impact to the user community.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.1.3 - Define and create deployment procedure workflow</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Outlining processes, methods, and equipment for deployment of IT solutions. Manage core operations servers like subversion server, production server, and development server, to make the IT services and solutions available for internal/client use.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.1.4 - Define IT change/release standards</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Establishing guidelines for the changed/released IT services and solutions to meet business objectives with optimum utilization. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.1.5 - Assign deployment approval responsibilities</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Coordinating development approval responsibilities based on defined change standards.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.1.6 - Analyze deployments outcomes</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Evaluating the impact (pros and cons) of IT services deployment.


</details>

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.2.6 - Create implementation communications</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Coordinating change implementation in IT services and solutions communications with employees and stakeholders. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.2.7 - Manage IT roll-back procedures</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Managing procedures to return to initial pre-deployment stage or previous state from current environment.


</details>

**PCF ID:** 20858

**Definition:** Strategizing and executing changes in IT solutions and services. Create a plan for deploying the changes. Communicate with stakeholders about the changes. Administer and implement the changes. Train the resources who will be affected by these changes. Install changes and verify their effect.

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.5.1 - Conduct IT training</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Preparing users for changes in IT solutions. Conduct training sessions and engagement activities to familiarize users with the new changes. Implementing the programs for training IT employees.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.5.2 - Prepare and distribute service/solution communications</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Coordinating communications regarding the changes in IT services and solutions with employees in the organization.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.5.3 - Support organizational changes</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating a strategy for providing support for organizational changes. Providing support to users of IT services and solutions.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.5.4 - Execute rollout plans</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Executing a plan for introducing the IT services and solutions to the organization's end user base.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.5.5 - Provide rollout support</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Establishing services for providing support to users of IT services and solutions for rollout. Define the plethora of services by which the organization assists users of technology products.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.5.6 - Manage rollout support capabilities</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Managing the necessary skills and competencies required to efficiently provide IT resolution for rollout through the support structure. Identify the gaps and needs in support structure.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>8.6.5.7 - Monitor and record rollout issues</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Track and record any issues being faced due to rollout. Define methodology of assessment for measuring and monitoring issues.


</details>
