# 7.7 - Manage employee information and analytics

> **PCF ID:** 17056 | **Elements:** 8 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 7

## Definition

Managing the employee reporting processes, employee inquiry process, employee information and data, and the HR information systems. Create and administer the employee metrics. Develop and handle the time and attendance systems. Refurbish the indicators for employee retention and motivation.

## Overview

This process group covers **manage employee information and analytics** within the broader context of Develop and Manage Human Capital.

### 7.7.1 - Manage reporting processes

**PCF ID:** 10522

**Definition:** Providing information and reports regarding employees to management.


### 7.7.2 - Manage employee inquiry process

**PCF ID:** 10523

**Definition:** Handling instances where an employee believes that he/she has been inappropriately treated or he/she desires clarification. Encourage employees to inquire when needed. Record and clarify the issues for which the enquiry has been made.


### 7.7.3 - Manage and maintain employee data

**PCF ID:** 10524

**Definition:** Capturing and updating employee information and data and information on the employees. 


### 7.7.4 - Manage human resource information systems HRIS

**PCF ID:** 10525

**Definition:** Administering and maintaining HR information systems that take care of activities related to HR, accounting, management, and payroll.


### 7.7.5 - Develop and manage employee metrics

**PCF ID:** 10526

**Definition:** Creating and maintaining performance metrics for employees. Create and manage a strategic system of data and statistics to accurately gauge each employee's information. Consider productivity metrics, efficiency metrics, training metrics, etc.


### 7.7.6 - Develop and manage time and attendance systems

**PCF ID:** 10527

**Definition:** Developing and maintaining systems for managing the time and attendance of employees. Routinely upgrade the process and systems that track when employees start and stop work, the department where the work is performed, attendance in addition to tracking meals and breaks, the type of work performed, and the number of items produced.


### 7.7.7 - Manage/Collect employee suggestions and perform employee research

**PCF ID:** 10530

**Definition:** Procuring and handling suggestions from employees, and performing research on employees. Manage and analyze the programs that help the organization to tap into employee ideas for improving the organization's processes and/or products. Use surveys, focus groups, and other data-gathering methods to find out the attitudes, opinions, and feelings of members of an organization.
