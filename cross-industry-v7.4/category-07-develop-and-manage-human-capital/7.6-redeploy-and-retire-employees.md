# 7.6 - Redeploy and retire employees

> **PCF ID:** 10413 | **Elements:** 11 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 4

## Definition

Managing the reassignment and retirement of employees. Manage the process of employee promotion and demotion. Administer separation, retirement, and leaves of absence. Outplace employees. Deploy personnel. Relocate employees in order to manage assignments.

## Overview

This process group covers **redeploy and retire employees** within the broader context of Develop and Manage Human Capital.

### 7.6.1 - Manage promotion and demotion process

**PCF ID:** 10512

**Definition:** Administering the process of promoting and demoting employees. Design a system for advancing or demoting an employee's rank or position. Leverage techniques such as horizontal promotion, vertical promotion, dry promotion, and involuntary/voluntary demotion.


### 7.6.2 - Manage separation

**PCF ID:** 10513

**Definition:** Managing the process of employee separation, including resignations, discharges, and layoffs. Inform the employee of the termination. Complete paperwork for continuation of benefits.


### 7.6.3 - Manage retirement

**PCF ID:** 10514

**Definition:** Managing and administering instances where a person stops employment completely.


### 7.6.4 - Manage leave of absence

**PCF ID:** 10515

**Definition:** Managing the period of time that an employee must be away from their primary job, while maintaining the status of employee (i.e., paid and unpaid leave of absence but not vacations, holidays, hiatuses, sabbaticals, and work-from-home programs).


### 7.6.5 - Develop and implement employee outplacement

**PCF ID:** 10516

**Definition:** Helping former employees transition to new jobs or to re-orient themselves in the job market. Deliver help through one-on-one sessions or in a group format. Provide guidance in career evaluation, resume writing, interview preparation, developing networks, and job searching.


### 7.6.6 - Manage workforce scheduling

**PCF ID:** 20132

**Definition:** Organizing the workforce so that all positions are covered for all shifts with the necessary skilled resources in place. Have a system in place to backfill positions while an employee is on leave.

### 7.6.7 - Relocate employees and manage assignments

**PCF ID:** 17055

**Definition:** Managing the relocation of employees in order to carry out assignments. Manage internal business processes to transfer employees, their families, and/or entire departments of a business to a new location.
