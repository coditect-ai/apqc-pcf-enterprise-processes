# 7.5 - Reward and retain employees

> **PCF ID:** 10412 | **Elements:** 19 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 4

## Definition

Creating frameworks for rewarding and recognizing employees with the objective of retaining them. Create and manage programs for provision of rewards, recognition, and motivation. Manage and administer the benefits for employees. Help assist and retain employees. Administer payroll to employees.

## Overview

This process group covers **reward and retain employees** within the broader context of Develop and Manage Human Capital.

### 7.5.1 - Develop and manage reward, recognition, and motivation programs

**PCF ID:** 10494

**Definition:** Developing a salary/compensation structure and plan; developing a benefits and reward plan; develop commission plan; performing competitive analyses of benefits and rewards; identifying compensation requirements based on compensation, benefits, and HR policies; administering compensation, commission, and rewards to employees; and rewarding and motivating employees.

| 7.5.1.4 | Identify compensation requirements based on financial, benefits, and HR policies | 10501 | 0 | N |
| 7.5.1.5 | Administer compensation and rewards to employees | 10502 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.1.1 - Develop salary/compensation structure and plan</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating the framework for the provision of salary/compensation to employees. Break down the salary structure into different components such as fixed pay, variable pay, bonus, and allowances such medical allowance, and rent allowance, etc. Develop, adjust, and maintain a pay structure.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.1.2 - Develop benefits and reward plan</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Developing a plan for provision of rewards, commission, and benefits to employees. Plan health benefits, retirement benefits, non-monetary benefits, etc.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.1.3 - Perform competitive analysis of benefit and rewards</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Analyzing and evaluating the organization's benefits and rewards plan. Compare/Benchmark the benefits and employees plan with other organizations to adhere to industry standard practices.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.1.4 - Identify compensation requirements based on financial, benefits, and HR policies</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Recognizing the employee requirements for compensation on the basis of the financial, benefits, and HR policies of the organization. Recognize individual compensation requirements regarding the financial policies of the organization. Consider the benefits plan and overall HR policies while selecting compensation requirements.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.1.5 - Administer compensation and rewards to employees</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Managing the provision of compensations and rewards to the employees while maintaining consistency with the compensation and benefits plan. Follow the compensation and benefits plan rigorously in order to avoid any discrepancies.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.1.6 - Reward and motivate employees</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Rewarding and stimulating the performance efforts of employees. Create methods for motivating employees. Spur extrinsic and intrinsic motivation.


</details>


### 7.5.2 - Manage and administer benefits

**PCF ID:** 10495

**Definition:** Managing and ensuring benefits enrollment by the employees. Process any benefit claims made by the employees. Balance the estimated amount and entitled amount of benefits.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 7.5.2.1 | Deliver employee benefits program | 10504 | 0 | N |
| 7.5.2.2 | Administer benefit enrollment | 10505 | 0 | N |
| 7.5.2.3 | Process claims | 10506 | 0 | N |
| 7.5.2.4 | Perform benefit reconciliation | 10507 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.2.1 - Deliver employee benefits program</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Implementing the programs that specify employee benefits, other than salary provided, such as those concerning medical care, death, and disability.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.2.2 - Administer benefit enrollment</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Handling the employee enrollment for obtaining benefits. Manage employee enrollment and eligibility. Encourage employees to enroll for benefits.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.2.3 - Process claims</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Processing any formal requests or demands made by the employees claiming that they have earned some benefits. Send the request further up the managerial hierarchy to ensure approval.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.2.4 - Perform benefit reconciliation</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Carrying out reconciliation of benefits delivered to employees. Compare the estimated benefit requirement made by the employee and the actual amount of benefits the employee is entitled to receive.


</details>


### 7.5.3 - Manage employee assistance and retention

**PCF ID:** 10496

**Definition:** Managing activities centered around delivering programs to support work/life balance for employees; developing family support systems; reviewing retention and motivation indicators; and reviewing compensation plans.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 7.5.3.1 | Deliver programs to support work/life balance for employees | 10508 | 0 | N |
| 7.5.3.2 | Develop family support systems | 10509 | 0 | N |
| 7.5.3.3 | Review retention and motivation indicators | 10510 | 0 | N |
| 7.5.3.4 | Review compensation plan | 10511 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.3.1 - Deliver programs to support work/life balance for employees</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Designing programs that prompt proper balance between work (i.e., career and ambition) and lifestyle (i.e., health, pleasure, leisure, family, and spiritual development/meditation). Account for dependent care, flexible working arrangements, leaves of absence, on-the-job training, etc.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.3.2 - Develop family support systems</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating a support structure that aligns with local and federal laws that allow for support for families. This could include things like maternity leave, care for a family member, or in some cases, extended sick leave.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.3.3 - Review retention and motivation indicators</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Reassessing the indicators for retention and motivation of employees. Monitor the indicators that signal the levels of motivation and retention. Regularly update and upgrade indicators to avoid depreciation and ensure high efficiency.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.5.3.4 - Review compensation plan</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Analyzing existing compensation plans and making changes necessary to continue to retain employees. 


</details>


### 7.5.4 - Administer payroll

**PCF ID:** 10497

**Definition:** Managing the sum of all financial records of salaries for an employee, including wages, bonuses, and deductions. Use a payroll management system to deal with the financial aspects of employees' salaries, allowances, deductions, gross pay, net pay, etc. Generate pay slips for a specific period.
