# 7.3 - Manage employee on boarding, development, and training

> **PCF ID:** 20599 | **Elements:** 26 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 6

## Definition

Assisting employees in developing their capabilities, and providing them counseling services. Handle the orientation and deployment of the employees. Administer the performance of employees. Administer the development and enhancement of the employees. Provide training and development programs for employees.

## Overview

This process group covers **manage employee on boarding, development, and training** within the broader context of Develop and Manage Human Capital.

### 7.3.1 - Manage employee orientation and deployment

**PCF ID:** 10469

**Definition:** Creating and maintaining various employee on-boarding programs typically known as induction programs in order to ensure that the new employees are effectively introduced to the organization and its existing employees. Examine and evaluate the performance of these induction programs. Execute these programs on the ground level.

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.1.1 - Create/maintain employee on-boarding program</strong> (2 tasks)</summary>

**Definition:** Creating and maintaining a mechanism through which new employees acquire the necessary knowledge, skills, and behaviors to become effective organizational members and insiders. Conduct formal meetings, lectures, videos, printed materials, and/or computer-based orientations to introduce newcomers to their new jobs and the organization.

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.1.2 - Evaluate the effectiveness of the employee on-boarding program</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Assessing the performance and effectiveness of employee on-boarding program. Examine the performance of on-boarding program through feedback and reviews from the new employees. Create web and written forms. Obtain information through face-to-face discussions.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.1.3 - Execute on-boarding program</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Bringing the employee on-boarding program into effect. Implement Create/Maintain employee on-boarding program [10474]. Conduct training sessions and employee engagement programs.


</details>


### 7.3.2 - Manage employee performance

**PCF ID:** 10470

**Definition:** Defining individual performance objectives. Review performance in order to provide appraisals. Evaluate the efficiency and effectiveness of the current performance program. Update it regularly.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 7.3.2.1 | Define employee performance objectives | 10479 | 0 | N |
| 7.3.2.2 | Review, appraise, and manage employee performance | 10480 | 0 | N |
| 7.3.2.3 | Evaluate and review performance program | 10481 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.2.1 - Define employee performance objectives</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Outlining the objectives for employee performance. Establish key performance objectives and measures such as customer-focus objectives, financially focused objectives, and employee growth objectives.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.2.2 - Review, appraise, and manage employee performance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Refurbishing, appraising, and managing the performance of employees. Create performance reviews for all the employees by qualitatively and quantitatively measuring them. Use the reviews to provide performance appraisals. Monitor under-performing employees.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.2.3 - Evaluate and review performance program</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Assessing and revamping performance programs, including the instruments used to measure employee performance standards. Review and upgrade these performance programs to avoid any deprivations and ensure effectiveness.


</details>


### 7.3.3 - Manage employee development

**PCF ID:** 10472

**Definition:** Establishing employee development guidelines. Lay out career paths and plans for them. Manage the development of their skills to enhance their skills, ability, and knowledge.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 7.3.3.1 | Define employee development guidelines | 10487 | 0 | N |
| 7.3.3.2 | Develop employee career plans and career paths | 10488 | 0 | N |
| 7.3.3.3 | Manage employee skill and competency development | 17051 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.3.1 - Define employee development guidelines</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Outlining the guidelines for development of employees. Design development policies and procedures to identify areas of growth for employees, either in their current position or in preparation for future roles. Include topics related to knowledge and skill development.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.3.2 - Develop employee career plans and career paths</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Designing a future career path for the employees that encourages them to explore and gather information. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.3.3 - Manage employee skill and competency development</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Administering the development of employee skills. Conduct training, coaching and mentoring, job-rotation and cross training, lateral moves, etc.


</details>


### 7.3.4 - Develop and train employees

**PCF ID:** 10473

**Definition:** Creating a link between employee and organizational development needs. Conduct and manage employee training programs by considering the need and availability of these programs.

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.4.1 - Align employee with organization development needs</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Aligning the needs of the employees to development needs.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.4.2 - Define employee competencies</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Defining the skills, knowledge, abilities, and attributes needed to carry out a specific job.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.4.3 - Align learning programs with competencies</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Aligning the learning programs with the core capabilities and competencies of the organization. Contextualize the training programs so that employees can expand their knowledge base and add new skills in line with the core competencies of the organization.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.4.4 - Establish training needs by analysis of required and available skills</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Determining the training necessitated by business processes, using an examination of skill sets that are needed by the organization and those already possessed. Examine the various skills required by individual employees. Design training in light of the availability of resources to provide specific segments of training.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>7.3.4.5 - Develop, conduct, and manage employee and/or management training programs</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating, implementing, and managing the programs for training employees. Create and design sessions on the basis of the needs and the availability of the skills. Conduct the sessions on the ground. Manage all aspects related to the training programs. Consider including literacy training, interpersonal skills training, technical training, problem-solving training, diversity or sensitivity training, etc.


</details>

<summary><strong>7.3.4.6 - Manage examinations and certifications</strong> (4 tasks)</summary>

**Definition:** Managing identified training programs for employees. Engage with industries to provide certifications, administer certification test, and maintain active certification.
