# 7.8 - Manage employee communication

> **PCF ID:** 17057 | **Elements:** 3 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 1

## Definition

Creating an effective plan that initiates and promotes communication and engagement among the employees and between employees and management.

## Overview

This process group covers **manage employee communication** within the broader context of Develop and Manage Human Capital.

### 7.8.1 - Develop employee communication plan

**PCF ID:** 10529

**Definition:** Creating a plan for managing communication among employees. Inform employees of direction. Counter resistance with change management approaches. Seek specific areas of input to the decision-making process. Seek varying degrees of involvement and co-creation.


### 7.8.2 - Conduct employee engagement surveys

**PCF ID:** 16944

**Definition:** Questioning employees to ascertain overall workplace satisfaction.
