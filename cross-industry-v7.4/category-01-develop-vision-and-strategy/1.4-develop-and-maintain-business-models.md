# 1.4 - Develop and maintain business models

> **PCF ID:** 20944 | **Elements:** 12 | **Metrics Available:** N | **Benchmarkable:** 0

## Definition

Establishing how an organization creates, delivers and captures value or makes profit. Identify the products or services that a business will sell, its target market, anticipated expenses, and other core aspects of its modus operandi. Revise the plan as required to reflect changing circumstances.

## Overview

This process group covers **develop and maintain business models** within the broader context of Develop Vision and Strategy.

### 1.4.1 - Develop business models

**PCF ID:** 20945

**Definition:** Creating an economic model that describes the goals of an organization and the business processes needed to achieve those goals. This involves information gathering, securing necessary approvals and authorizations, integrating with preexisting models, including the general business concept [10002] and business strategy [10015], and formally accepting the model as the basis for organization's day-to-day operations.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|

| 1.4.1.2 | Secure appropriate approvals | 20947 | 0 | N |

| 1.4.1.4 | Adopt the business model | 20949 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>1.4.1.1 - Assemble business model information</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Collecting all relevant materials needed to develop the business model, so that it can adequately model its processes.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.4.1.2 - Secure appropriate approvals</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Obtaining required permissions, licenses and authorizations that legitimize the business, help to mitigate associated risks and safeguard the operations.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.4.1.3 - Identify integration points with existing models</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Ensuring coherence with pre-exsiting models to avoid contradictions between models. Make sure that all models represent the same long-term vision.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.4.1.4 - Adopt the business model</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Consenting to a particular business model and formally accepting it to serve as the set of guiding principles in operating the company.


</details>


### 1.4.2 - Maintain business models

**PCF ID:** 20950

**Definition:** Revising and updating business models to reflect the changes in the marketed services, product inventory, market behavior, available resources or accrued expenses. Determine how and when to modify the accepted business model in response to incoming feedback.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 1.4.2.1 | Establish business model maintenance parameters | 20951 | 0 | N |
| 1.4.2.2 | Accept business model feedback parameters | 20952 | 0 | N |
| 1.4.2.3 | Prioritize and manage incoming feedback | 20953 | 0 | N |
| 1.4.2.4 | Update existing models | 20954 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>1.4.2.1 - Establish business model maintenance parameters</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Determining the timeline, procedures and responsibilities for reviewing the business model and for updating it to best serve the organization.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.4.2.2 - Accept business model feedback parameters</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Deciding the type of responses, reactions, sentiments and insights that are crucial to be taken into consideration with business model maintenance.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.4.2.3 - Prioritize and manage incoming feedback</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Evaluating the feedback regarding products, services, processes or resources. Determine which judgments are critical and mandate changes to the current business model to better deliver the desired value 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>1.4.2.4 - Update existing models</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Modifying the business models that are presently in use in response to incoming feedback or changing markets to achieve the enterprise business goals.


</details>


### 1.4.3 - Establish business model governance

**PCF ID:** 20955

**Definition:** Creating and implementing a strategy, responsibilities and control mechanisms for managing business models that are timely, efficient and cost-effective.
