# 12.5 - Manage public relations program

> **PCF ID:** 11014 | **Elements:** 6 | **Metrics Available:** N | **Benchmarkable:** 0

## Definition

Managing a public relations programs through business and communications skills. 

## Overview

This process group covers **manage public relations program** within the broader context of Manage External Relationships.

### 12.5.1 - Manage community relations

**PCF ID:** 11066

**Definition:** Developing and administering community relations. Establish business connections with the people constituting the environment the organization operates in and draws resources from in order to foster mutual understanding, trust, and support. Create programs that promote the organization's image in a positive and community-oriented way.


### 12.5.2 - Manage media relations

**PCF ID:** 11067

**Definition:** Developing and managing relations with media. Develop connections with journalists to solicit critical, third-party endorsements for a product, issue, service, or organization.


### 12.5.3 - Promote political stability

**PCF ID:** 11068

**Definition:** Promoting political security and stability in the regions where the organization conducts business. Encourage political stability in the regions where the organization operates. Support civic programs, citizen engagement, connection platforms, etc.


### 12.5.4 - Create press releases

**PCF ID:** 11069

**Definition:** Developing press releases to communicate developments and generate interest in the organization. 


### 12.5.5 - Issue press releases

**PCF ID:** 11070

**Definition:** Issuing press releases to carefully selected media in distribution channels such as the web, newspapers, and social media.
