# 12.2 - Manage government and industry relationships

> **PCF ID:** 11011 | **Elements:** 18 | **Metrics Available:** N | **Benchmarkable:** 0

## Definition

Creating and maintaining relationships with government and industry representatives. 

## Overview

This process group covers **manage government and industry relationships** within the broader context of Manage External Relationships.

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.1.1 - Assess relationships</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Ascertaining how the business entity relates to all levels of government. Identify areas that needs further growth and resources to foster those relationships.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.1.2 - Appoint responsible executives</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Assigning executive level resources to manage, grow, and drive relationships with government bodies.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.1.3 - Monitor relationships</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Analyzing current relationships with government bodies and entities. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.1.4 - Receive input from internal advisors</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Garnering internal advice from an informal group in order to successfully maintain and advance relationships.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.1.5 - Receive input from external advisors</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Garnering third party advice from an informal group in order to successfully maintain and advance relationships.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.1.6 - Liaise with authorities</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Meeting with government heads and representatives.


</details>


### 12.2.2 - Manage relations with quasi-government bodies

**PCF ID:** 11039

**Definition:** Managing relations with quasi-governmental organizations, corporations, businesses, or any other agency that is treated by national laws and principles to be under the supervision of the government but also distinct and self-directed from the government.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 12.2.2.1 | Establish relationships with agencies | 12875 | 0 | N |
| 12.2.2.2 | Respond to audit inquiries | 12876 | 0 | N |
| 12.2.2.3 | Maintain documentation of contacts | 12877 | 0 | N |
| 12.2.2.4 | Plan and manage meetings | 12878 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.2.1 - Establish relationships with agencies</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Engaging government, regulatory, or industry agencies to establish relationships. Analyze steps and requirements necessary for inclusion, if needed. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.2.2 - Respond to audit inquiries</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Cooperating with auditors regarding potential or ongoing inquiries.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.2.3 - Maintain documentation of contacts</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Keeping a repository of documents that contain information about the network of partners, prospects and customers. Keep records up-to-date with routine reviews and modify as needed.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.2.4 - Plan and manage meetings</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Ensuring regular interaction between business entity and quasi-government bodies in order to maintain established relationships. Collect and record meeting data for further analysis and use.


</details>


### 12.2.3 - Manage relations with trade or industry groups

**PCF ID:** 11040

**Definition:** Managing relations with organizations established and financed by businesses that operate in a specific industry. Participate in public relations actions such as lobbying and publishing, advertising, education, and political donations.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 12.2.3.1 | Evaluate the requirements for strategic relationships | 12879 | 0 | N |
| 12.2.3.2 | Monitor the success of the partnerships | 12880 | 0 | N |
| 12.2.3.3 | Extend or change the relationships | 12881 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.3.1 - Evaluate the requirements for strategic relationships</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Determining the requirements to enter in to an agreement with trade or industry agencies. Discover what activities or processes can be conducted to provide the best mutual outcome.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.3.2 - Monitor the success of the partnerships</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Analyzing current relationships with trade and industry groups. Ensure that the partnership in successful and make modifications where needed. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>12.2.3.3 - Extend or change the relationships</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Providing additional information or inclusion for third party trade or industry entities; or changing existing parameters to modify the current relationship. Communicate and execute changes.


</details>


### 12.2.4 - Manage lobby activities

**PCF ID:** 11041

**Definition:** Managing lobbying activities to affect government policies.
