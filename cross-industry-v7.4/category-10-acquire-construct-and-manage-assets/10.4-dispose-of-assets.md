# 10.4 - Dispose of assets

> **PCF ID:** 10940 | **Elements:** 6 | **Metrics Available:** Y | **Benchmarkable:** 1

## Definition

Retiring productive and non-productive assets. 

## Overview

This process group covers **dispose of assets** within the broader context of Acquire, Construct, and Manage Assets.

### 10.4.1 - Develop exit strategy

**PCF ID:** 10952

**Definition:** Creating a strategy for managing asset exits. 


### 10.4.2 - Decommission productive assets

**PCF ID:** 19258

**Definition:** Retiring assets that are no longer viable to the business. Decommission assets that are no longer in working order, are out of date, or whose maintenance exceeds the cost of replacement.


### 10.4.3 - Perform sale or trade

**PCF ID:** 10953

**Definition:** Performing the sale of assets. Achieve and complete the sale process. Deliver the end product to the customers. 


### 10.4.4 - Perform abandonment

**PCF ID:** 10954

**Definition:** Abandoning assets. Manage the act of deliberately and permanently giving up, granting, leaving, or surrendering property or buildings and allowing others to use the intellectual property without protest.


### 10.4.5 - Perform waste and hazardous goods management

**PCF ID:** 16970

**Definition:** Disposing of waste and hazardous materials as outlined by state, local, and federal regulations.
