# 10.1 - Plan and acquire assets

> **PCF ID:** 10937 | **Elements:** 17 | **Metrics Available:** N | **Benchmarkable:** 2

## Definition

Building and purchasing non-productive assets for the organization. Acquire and construct non-productive assets that are not yielding any income/profits to the business. 

## Overview

This process group covers **plan and acquire assets** within the broader context of Acquire, Construct, and Manage Assets.

### 10.1.1 - Develop property strategy and long term vision

**PCF ID:** 10941

**Definition:** Strategizing a long-term vision for managing properties. Prepare strategies and a long-term vision for managing purchased/retained properties.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 10.1.1.1 | Confirm alignment of property requirements with business strategy | 10955 | 0 | Y |
| 10.1.1.2 | Appraise the external environment | 10956 | 0 | N |
| 10.1.1.3 | Determine build or buy decision | 10957 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.1.1 - Confirm alignment of property requirements with business strategy</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating alignment between the requirement of properties and the overall business strategy. This process requires the organization to align the requirement of properties in accordance with its business strategies of the organization.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.1.2 - Appraise the external environment</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Evaluating the impact of the external environment. Evaluate the circumstances, objects, events, and aspects surrounding an organization that affects its actions and selections and that recognizes its opportunities and risks.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.1.3 - Determine build or buy decision</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Deciding whether to buy or build properties. Study the market forces about property prices and cost of construction in order to take decisions based on the market research.


</details>

**PCF ID:** 10943

**Definition:** Recognizing the needs of facility users in order to construct a project proposal that meets those needs.

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.2.1 - Design facility</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Preparing and analyzing different designs for a facility in order to finalize which design will be the most suitable option.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.2.2 - Analyze budget</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Evaluating the feasibility of budgets prepared for the construction of facilities.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.2.3 - Select property</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Assessing and choosing the appropriate property. Analyze the property requirements. Review the available property options. Finalize the most suitable option.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.2.4 - Negotiate terms for facility</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Discussing the terms and conditions of facilities to be occupied according to the business requirements and availability of budgets.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.2.5 - Manage construction or modification to building</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Constructing the buildings. Manage renovations according to requirements and demands. 


</details>


### 10.1.3 - Provide workspace and facilities

**PCF ID:** 10944

**Definition:** Managing the provision of the workspace and its assets. Arrange an office space with all assets (tables, chairs, computers, admin staff, etc.) according to requirements.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 10.1.3.1 | Acquire workspace and facilities | 10963 | 0 | N |
| 10.1.3.2 | Change fit/form/function of workspace and facilities | 10964 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.3.1 - Acquire workspace and facilities</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Attaining the office space with all assets (tables, chairs, computers, admin staff, etc.) according to requirements.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.3.2 - Change fit/form/function of workspace and facilities</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Modifying the formation of the workspace and its assets. Make necessary changes in an office space with all assets (tables, chairs, computers, admin staff, interior designing, etc.) according to requirements.


</details>

**Definition:** Managing all operational activities of the facility. Manage how each function/business unit works. Support the manufacturing facility to attain organizational goals.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.1.4.2 - Relocate material and tools</strong></summary>

</details>
