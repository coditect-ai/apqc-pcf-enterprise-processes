# 10.3 - Maintain productive assets

> **PCF ID:** 19238 | **Elements:** 23 | **Metrics Available:** N | **Benchmarkable:** 0

## Definition

Preserving productive assets through the planning, managing, and performance of preventative, routine, and critical maintenance work. 

## Overview

This process group covers **maintain productive assets** within the broader context of Acquire, Construct, and Manage Assets.

### 10.3.1 - Plan asset maintenance

**PCF ID:** 19239

**Definition:** Ensuring that necessary resources are available and tasks are prioritized accordingly through planning. Provide strategies and policies that identify tasks that need to be completed, and the resources necessary to fulfill those tasks.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 10.3.1.1 | Develop maintenance strategies | 19240 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.1.2 | Analyze assets and predict maintenance requirements | 10967 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.1.3 | Specify maintenance policies | 19241 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.1.4 | Integrate preventive maintenance into operations schedule | 10968 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.1.5 | Identify work management tasks & priorities | 19242 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.1.6 | Conduct resource planning | 19243 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.1.7 | Create work plans | 19244 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.1.1 - Develop maintenance strategies</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating goals and agendas to better realize the success of the maintenance policies that have been put into place.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.1.2 - Analyze assets and predict maintenance requirements</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Evaluating assets in order to project future requirements for maintenance. Evaluate the present working condition of assets. Determine the future maintenance requirements of assets.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.1.3 - Specify maintenance policies</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Communicating policies in regards to asset maintenance. Provide a clear set of procedures and policies that outline what will be involved in the maintenance process. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.1.4 - Integrate preventive maintenance into operations schedule</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Devising a methodology and procedure for assimilating the works of planned maintenance into the schedule scheme for the processing of finished products that utilizes the same machinery. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.1.5 - Identify work management tasks & priorities</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Identifying the steps needed for asset maintenance. List out the those tasks that are involved in completing this process. Prioritize the tasks that are created.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.1.6 - Conduct resource planning</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Analyzing workload needs in relation to asset maintenance and plan resources around those needs.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.1.7 - Create work plans</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Creating procedures on how to maintain productive assets. 


</details>


### 10.3.2 - Manage asset maintenance

**PCF ID:** 19245

**Definition:** Ensuring that asset maintenance is conducted in a timely manner and successfully. Schedule work with the required resources with an eye on quality control and safety. Verify that contracted maintenance meets performance targets.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 10.3.2.1 | Schedule maintenance work | 19246 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.2.2 | Obtain required resources | 19247 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.2.3 | Undertake quality control | 19248 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.2.4 | Update work and asset records | 19249 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.2.5 | Manage maintenance work safety | 19250 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.2.6 | Define maintenance performance targets | 19251 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.2.7 | Monitor maintenance performance against targets/contracts | 19252 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.2.1 - Schedule maintenance work</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Defining a timetable for which to execute the maintenance of the asset. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.2.2 - Obtain required resources</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Gathering resources needed to complete all maintenance work. Verify that all resources have the proper training and skills to perform the work.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.2.3 - Undertake quality control</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Implementing a checks and balances system to verify that the maintenance was performed correctly. Rework when errors are found. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.2.4 - Update work and asset records</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Modifying existing maintenance records to include all new work that has been performed, what assets were serviced, and any issues that might have arisen. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.2.5 - Manage maintenance work safety</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Assuring that all safety laws and regulations are being implemented and followed. Align practices with regulatory bureaus such as OSHA , EHS, and ISO. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.2.6 - Define maintenance performance targets</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Outlining what should be achieved through predictive indicators with regard to performing maintenance. This could include the length of time it takes to perform routine maintenance or how often unplanned maintenance feasibly occur.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.2.7 - Monitor maintenance performance against targets/contracts</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Following set performance targets, monitor and gage the success of the organization in meeting those targets.


</details>


### 10.3.3 - Perform asset maintenance

**PCF ID:** 19253

**Definition:** Engaging in all aspects of asset maintenance. Ensure that all assets are functioning properly and to all specified codes and regulations where applicable. Maintenance includes all preventative, routine, and corrective activates.

| ID | Activity | PCF ID | Tasks | Metrics |
|----|----------|--------|-------|--------|
| 10.3.3.1 | Perform preventative asset maintenance | 10947 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.3.2 | Perform routine asset maintenance | 19254 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.3.3 | Perform corrective asset maintenance and repairs | 19255 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.3.4 | Identify unplanned maintenance requirements | 19256 | 0 | N |
| 10.3.3.5 | Perform unplanned maintenance and repairs | 19257 | 0 | N |

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.3.1 - Perform preventative asset maintenance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Performing prophylactic maintenance in an effort to avoid corrective or unplanned repairs.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.3.2 - Perform routine asset maintenance</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Carrying out required maintenance to continue upkeep of equipment or assets. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.3.3 - Perform corrective asset maintenance and repairs</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Repairing or correcting faults that occur with an asset. This could be a break or other repairable damage. 


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.3.4 - Identify unplanned maintenance requirements</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Realizing potential or current problems with assets that would require unplanned maintenance. Unplanned maintenance is a repair or change that needs to be made that is not preventative or routine.


</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>10.3.3.5 - Perform unplanned maintenance and repairs</strong></summary>

**Definition:** Performing repairs that occur outside of normal routine or preventative maintenance. 


</details>
